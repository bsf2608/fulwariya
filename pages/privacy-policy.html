Privacy Policy

Your trust is our most valuable asset. Learn how we handle your data across all Fulwariya Tech apps.

Fulwariya Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") operates several apps, including but not limited to SurveyOg and our Games Portal. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, fill out a survey, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, and any data captured during survey activities (like GPS coordinates for SurveyOg).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, communicate with you, and ensure the security of our platform. For SurveyOg, GPS data is used solely for the purpose of verifying data collection locations as requested by your survey projects.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All survey responses in SurveyOg are encrypted during transit and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with third-party service providers (like Firebase or hosting providers) to the extent necessary to operate our apps.
