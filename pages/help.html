Software Features & Uses

FINBI Offline - GST Billing

FINBI Offline acts as a zero-latency desktop engine. You can configure up to 5 individual tax slabs (0%, 3%, 5%, 18%, 40%) per product. To generate an invoice, simply tap 'New Bill', scan a barcode, and the CGST/SGST drops directly into your GSTR-1 sheet automatically.

Role Based Access (RBAC)

If you are deploying FINBI SaaS or SurveyOg to multiple employees, you can restrict permissions. Go to Settings > Users. Admin accounts have full ledger access, while Cashier accounts can only process POS transactions without viewing the P&L reports.

Cloud Connectivity

Need to back up your offline database? Link your FINBI Desktop instance to the app.fulwariya.in portal via the "Sync" menu. Your encrypted SQLite file will safely transmit to the Cloudflare-tunneled backend nodes.
