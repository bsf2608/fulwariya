What's included

Everything your business needs

From daily billing to year-end compliance — one app, no recurring fees.

📒

Double-Entry Accounting

Full chart of accounts, journal vouchers, trial balance, P&L and balance sheet — automatically maintained with Dr=Cr guaranteed.

🇮🇳

GST Compliance

GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, e-invoice generation, HSN summary, ITC tracking. Supports 0%, 3%, 5%, 18%, 40% slabs with CGST/SGST/IGST auto-split.

🖥️

Point of Sale (POS)

Fast retail billing with shift management, cash reconciliation, barcode scanning, and automatic stock deduction.

📦

Inventory Management

Multi-warehouse stock tracking, reorder levels, stock adjustments, average cost valuation, and stock movement reports.

👥

Multi-User RBAC

Add unlimited users with role-based permissions. Admin, Accountant, Cashier, and Viewer roles with granular module-level access control.

📶

Offline-First

100% offline — all data stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database. No internet required. Works in areas with poor connectivity.

💰

Payroll & HR

Employee management, salary structures, payroll runs, payslip generation — fully integrated with the accounts ledger.

🏭

Manufacturing / BOM

Bill of materials, production orders, raw material consumption, and finished goods stock updates — fully traceable.

📊

Financial Reports

Trial balance, P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, day book, AR/AP aging — export to PDF or Excel with one click.
